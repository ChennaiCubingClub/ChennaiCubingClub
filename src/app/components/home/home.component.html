Who are we?

  • We all have heard of Rubik's Cube. There is this competitive sport built around this puzzle known as Speedcubing. The World Cube Association (WCA) is the official world-wide body organizing standardized speedcubing competitions. The WCA approves regional organizations to carry out the same in specific regions. We are the official Speedcubing club of Tamil Nadu.

Why we exist?

  • We, Chennai Cubing Club, are here to increase the number and quality of competitions by uniting all the separate organization entities and make our state the best place to attend a Speedcubing competition. We are here to share everything about this amazing mental sport of Speedcubing to those of you who are yet to have experienced what it is like to do something that exercises both the jock and the nerd sides that you possess!

Why should you be a part of us?

  • This is the place where you can get to know about every competition or workshop that will take place all around Tamil Nadu. Also, if you have just discovered about the Speedcubing movement and are exploring ways to understand and learn to solve a Rubik's Cube, Congratulations; you have reached the right place to start your Speedcubing journey! Just hang on to us, we will take care of the rest.
