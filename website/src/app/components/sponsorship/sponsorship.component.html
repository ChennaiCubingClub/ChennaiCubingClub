  • Sponsorship

    Speedcubing competition will be a great place with minimum investment and maximum reach for Sponsors. Rubik’s Cubes and Speedcubing are now more popular than ever before. More and more speedcubers are regularly taking part in competitions in and outside their India. Which means that speedcubing is getting more and more attention and is becoming more visible. That makes it increasingly interesting for companies to sponsor competitions and/or participants. This will create great exposure and have a positive influence for and on your company/organization.

  • Who will be present in a speedcubing competition?
    Right from school going kids to college-goers to young working professionals actively compete. If your business is focused on school/college students or youth, then speedcubing competitions are the best places for you to be at.

    A lot of parents - Doctors, Lawyers, Business owners, Engineers; Our parents are from all walks of life. They come along with their kids and encourage them to participate. If your business is focused on an older set of people than children and youth, you have got that here as well.

    To sum up, a speedcubing competition is a place with an amazing mixture of people from different walks and stages of life, so, you find your right crowd anyway!
